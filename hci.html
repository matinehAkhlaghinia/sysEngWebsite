HCI Analysis

Personas

The team wanted to have a better idea of the clients and the types of people who would be using our bot and as a result in order to inform our design we had creates a few personas which would outline the behaviour and motivations of some of the potential users of the final bot.


Persona 1

Name: Nodoka Mitake

Type: Female

Role: Professor

About:

Professor Nodoka is a humanities professor for a college in the south of London. She is 60 years old and has just started going to the gym a few month ago. She does not use her phone a lot because she thinks technology is not for her. She likes to go the gym on a regular basis but often cannot attend classes because they are fully booked. She is very organised and likes her days to be planned out in advance so she can know what she is doing. She lives a bit far from the gym. She likes her job a lot and definitely not thinking about retiring.

Motivations:

  • Change her ways with technologies
  • Keep doing sports and stay in shape

Goals:

  • Find a way to plan her days where she goes to the gym better
  • Book gym classes quickly and get reminders when she has to go and on her calendar.

Pain Points:

  • She is not active on social media platforms and has very little technology at home
  • She likes things to be done quickly and does not want to waste too much time having to go on her phone to book a gym class.

Behaviour:

  • Motivation for sports and achievement of her goals: 8/10

    • 8
  • Use of messaging technology for her daily tasks: 2/10

    • 2
  • Loves trying new Technological tools: 1/10

    • 1
  • Interested in having things done quickly: 10/10

    • 10

Persona 2

Name: Mihai Breset

Type: Male

Role: Investment

About:

Mihai is an Investment banker living in London. He loves to work out and do sports. He is social and loves to meet new people. He is 40 years old and has a wife but no kids. He lives in the East part of the city with a really duplex apartment. He wants to keep living in london for the rest of his life and eventually create his own start-up in the fin-tech world. He is passionate about technology and always uses his phone. He has been going to the gym for 2 years now.

Motivations:

  • Loves sports and he likes to be coached by professionals
  • He enjoys meeting people that love sports as well.
  • He wants to remain fit and with an attractive body.

Goals:

  • Be able to book a gym class in less than a minute
  • He wants to go to the gym at least once a week
  • Not gain or lose any weight

Pain Points:

  • He has a very busy schedule
  • Hard for him sometimes to find motivation because he is very tired from his work
  • He is not a talented sportsman
  • Although he does not like to do that, he sometimes wants to cancel his gym classes in the last minute.

Behaviour:
