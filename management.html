Feedback


Over the course of the project we had held numerous meetings each week in order to make sure we were on track with our work and also to make sure we were making progress as per our timeline. While these meeting minutes can also be found in the biweekly reports, they are all listed here for convenience.

October

The meetings which occurred during October were mainly for choosing which project we would work on, setting up the initial tools for communication and also the second half was for the research we undertook and verifying our approaches in the meetings.

December

December meetings were mainly to finalise our proposals and meetings were based around collating the research done so we could start deciding how to implement our ideas. Also a few meetings were spent on deciding how to build up our project website.

November

The November meetings were mainly based around more focused research around the bots and digital assistants, as well as the architecture we would look to use. We also verified what tools we should be learning during these meetings.

January

Development began on the app and all team members began work on their respective parts - the bot, digital assistant and LUIS. Most of the intial meetings were related to setting up of the frameowrks and technical details.

February

Development continued and we met with representatives from Microsoft to discuss implementation of LUIS and other parts of the bot framework and also met to discuss further progress on the respective parts.

March

Most meetings in this period were related to finishing up loose ends and finalising the projects. This was mainly meetings with Alex and describing what had been achieved and also asking about any bugs which we had run into.

April

The meetings here were mainly to discuss whether the client was satisfied with the final deliverable.
