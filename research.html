Why we undertook research:

A significant portion of the first term has been spent on extensive research. The area of bots and digital assistants is relatively new when we consider the tools available to the public to build these. The project itself has a very specific aim when we consider the way the user interacts with the product as well as which platforms it will be best to implement on. As a result the team has done a lot of research to make the most informed decision to produce the best end product we can.

What we researched at a glance:

There have been several key areas to look into for the project ranging from the architecture, backend, bots, digital assistants to the front end design and UX and how these would be informed.
