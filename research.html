Why we undertook research:

A significant portion of the first term has been spent on extensive research. The area of bots and digital assistants is relatively new when we consider the tools available to the public to build these. The project itself has a very specific aim when we consider the way the user interacts with the product as well as which platforms it will be best to implement on. As a result the team has done a lot of research to make the most informed decision to produce the best end product we can.

Cortana

General info:

Cortana is a digital agent- the more you use Cortana, the more personalized your experience will be. You can either click on Cortana in your taskbar in Windows 10 or activate it by saying “Hey Cortana” on either a laptop/ desktop or mobile device.

Cortana is given a different personality in different regions and is not available in all countries where Windows 10 is supported.

Cortana can only be used on select platforms and devices natively and needs to be installed as an app for use on certain devices. As an app Cortana does function slightly more slowly as it does not have as much low-level access as digital assistants on their native devices however it works satisfactorily. The main cause for this is developer restrictions on IOS and Android devices.

If you make a request via speech or text that Cortana cannot answer or thinks you want to search - it will open up your default web browser and search the query using Bing (respects your default web browser but not your default search engine).
