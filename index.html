What is a Bot or Digital Assistant?

Digital Assistant:

A digital assistant is an application program that can understand natural language and complete electronic tasks for the end user. Digital Assistant respond to vocal request. Some existing digital assistants today are: Alexa, Cortana, Siri or Google assistant.

Chatbot:

A chatbot is a computer program designed to communicate with a user via text/messaging in order to complete a task. Chatbots are present on chatting platforms such as FB messenger or Skype. They respond to text/message request.

Abstract

Our team is working in conjunction with Alex from Nuffield and also Microsoft in order to develop a bot which will be deployed over various platforms such as Facebook Messenger and Skype in order to allow the customers of Nuffield Health to book gymn classes and eventually expanding to use other services provided by the company.

Our solution is a bot and digital assistant which allows for customers to better interact with the company and any bookings they wish to make. It incorporates many of Microsoft's products such as the Microsoft Bot Framework, LUIS and Azure while we also use Amazon's Echo and Alexa Skills Dev kit.

An achievement would be considered to be made when the Bot and Digitial Assitant are able to completed their required tasks under expected every day use and work reliably to process user intents to give a desired outcome.

Key Features

1 - Interact via a chatbot interface

User are able to interact with the project through a chatbot interface, they will be able to type queries directly into the chatbar with the bot and will receive responses based on their requests. This makes interacting with the project a very smooth experience overall and much faster for Nuffield users.

2 - View, Book and Cancel classes via chatbot

A user may book a class of their choice by giving the bot the name of the class and the time they wish to book for. Users can view all their current classes in a convenient message listing all their classes as cards (which have name, time and duration details). A user may also cancel a class they no longer wish to attend simply by chatting to the chatbot and specifying which class to cancel.

3 - Interact via a digital assistant

Currently if the user has an Amazon Echo or Dot, they will be able to sign in and interact with our product orally. This is a very convenient way for users to quickly find the status of classes or book gyms.

4 - View, Book and Cancel classes via digital assistant

In very much the same way, a user can complete all the aforementioned tasks that could be completed from the chatbot in Digital Assistant.
